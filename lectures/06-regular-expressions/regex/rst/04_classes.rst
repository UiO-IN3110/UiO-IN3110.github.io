Overview over character classes
===============================

--- ----------------- ------------
.   any character
\w  a word letter     [a-zA-Z0-9]
\W  not a word letter [^a-zA-Z0-9]
\d  a digit           [0-9]
\D  not a digit       [^0-9]
\s  a space           [ \t\n]
\S  not a space       [^ \t\n]
\n  new line
\t  tabular
--- ----------------- ------------
