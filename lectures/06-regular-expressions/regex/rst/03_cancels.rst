Cancelled Characters
====================

Literal versions of special characters has to be cancel.

Special characters::
     ^   [   .   $   {   *   (   \   +   )   |   ?   <   >
Written as::
    \^  \[  \.  \$  \{  \*  \(  \\  \+  \)  \|  \?  \<  \>

Special characters in character class::
     ^   -   ]
Written as::
    \^  \-  \]
